Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

still ("we", "our") is built with privacy as a core principle. We utilize Apple's iCloud services to ensure your data belongs solely to you.

1. Data Storage and iCloud

still does not maintain its own servers. All data you enter (including names, memorial dates, and target coordinates) is stored securely in your private iCloud Container via Apple's CloudKit framework.

This means:

  • Only you can access your data using your Apple ID.
  • We (the developers) have absolutely no access to your personal entries or saved coordinates.
  • Your data is automatically synced across your devices by Apple.

2. Location Services

To function as a compass, the app requires permission to access your device's location. This data is processed locally on your device in real-time to calculate the direction and distance to your saved coordinates.

Your current location is never uploaded to iCloud, our servers, or any third-party services. It is used strictly for the immediate calculation functionality.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not sell or share your data.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: sunyata-space@github.io
